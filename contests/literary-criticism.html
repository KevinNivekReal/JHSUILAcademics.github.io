Literary analyzation skills come into play in every career, in every field. For example, if you're into philosophy, archeology, or law, you'll have to try and decode ancient books, searching for something that might help you. Another instance is applying for a job, which will have you sign a contract that you will have to parse through to make sure you're not selling your soul.
