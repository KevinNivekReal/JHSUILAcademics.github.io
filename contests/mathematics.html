Official UIL Overview

This 40-minute, 60-question contest is designed to test knowledge and understanding in the areas of Algebra I and II, geometry, trigonometry, math analysis, analytic geometry, pre-calculus and elementary calculus.

Applications

With this contest, you can finally put to use all the math that you've learned throughout school and if you've forgotten anything, then practicing for this contest will help you remember it all over again. Knowing how to solve all these types of math problems is important because algebra equations are sneaky and like to surprise people.
