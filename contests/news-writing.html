Official UIL Overview

In this contest, you decide what’s fit to print as you make your way through a set of facts and quotes and pick out what’s important. You’ll work on deadline for the newspaper as you create a cohesive story that inquiring minds have a right to know.

Applications

News Writing is all about coming up with different ways of communicating an event or situation using evidence. This is a pretty clearly useful skill that can be used in a lot of different industries and in one’s personal life because it is a manner of communication that deals with laying out the facts of a specific situation.
