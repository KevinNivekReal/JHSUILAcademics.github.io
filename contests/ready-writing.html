Official UIL Overview

A short prompt will provide the inspiration for your creative ideas as you explore a topic or prove a point. Students write expository compositions that attempt to explain, prove or explore a topic in a balanced way, allowing the argument and the evidence given to be the deciding factor in the paper. Students are given a choice between two prompts, each an excerpt from literature, publications or speeches. The essay is judged on interest, organization and style.
