For Everybody.

UIL, University Interscholastic League, boasts several academic contests for every type of person. Whether you love Math or love public speaking, there is a contest for you.

Educational Competition.

UIL offers organized competitions between schools and districts. Experience the fun of competition while meeting new people from different backgrounds.

Easy To Join

To participate in any JHS UIL Academic contest, all you have to do is
1. Contact the contest’s coach
2. Attend a few meetings to practice some skills
3. Go to the competitions

And that’s it!
